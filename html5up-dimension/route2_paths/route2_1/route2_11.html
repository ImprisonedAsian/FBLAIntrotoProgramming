Perilous Path

Your instincts take over. You run.

The moment your feet slam against the metal floor, a screech erupts behind you—inhuman, distorted, filled with rage. The thing knows.

You don’t look back.

The corridor is a twisting maze of flickering lights and rusted doors, each one sealed shut. ECHO’s voice hisses in your ear, fractured but insistent.

“Escape is… not guaranteed.”

The lights flicker out.

Darkness swallows the hall, leaving only the sound of your own panicked breathing—and the relentless scraping behind you. The creature is moving fast.

Then, ahead—an intersection.

Your mind races. Left? Right? Straight?

The facility rumbles, and somewhere deep within, you hear the groan of metal twisting. The structure is failing.

ECHO’s voice crackles back to life. But this time, there’s something different. A sliver of urgency. Of fear.

"Only one path leads out."

The scraping behind you turns into a full sprint.

You have seconds. Decide.

Choice 1: Go left—A door marked "Emergency Exit," barely illuminated. But why hasn’t it opened automatically?

Choice 2: Go right—A dimly lit tunnel, old and unmarked. Something about it feels… off.

Choice 3: Go straight—A heavy blast door is at the end, slightly ajar, revealing a glimpse of open sky. But can you reach it in time?
