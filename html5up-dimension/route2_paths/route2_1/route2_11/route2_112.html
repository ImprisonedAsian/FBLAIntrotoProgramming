Weaving Veins

You don’t hesitate—diving into the narrow maintenance tunnels just as the creature’s screech rips through the air behind you.

The space is cramped, suffocating, with pipes lining the walls and rusted grates rattling beneath your hurried footsteps. The air is thick with the scent of metal and decay, and every breath feels heavier the deeper you go.

Behind you, the sound of twisting metal.

The creature is trying to force its way in.

You push forward, navigating the maze-like tunnels, your fingers grazing faded labels and scratched-out markings on the walls. Some lead to dead ends, others to sealed hatches, but one sign catches your eye:

"SERVICE ACCESS – CODE REQUIRED"

A hatch just ahead, slightly ajar. An escape?

Before you can reach it, a deep thud echoes behind you.

Your blood runs cold.

It’s inside the tunnels.

The chase isn’t over.

The creature is getting closer. You have seconds to decide.

Choice 1: Climb through the service hatch – If it opens, it might lead somewhere safer… or another dead end.

Choice 2: Keep running deeper into the tunnels – There might be another way out, but you’ll be venturing into complete darkness.
