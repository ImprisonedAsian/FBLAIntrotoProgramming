It Falls Into Place

You break through the rusted hinges easily.

With a thud, you land on the ground.

Scrambling to your feet, you jump away as the monster lunges at you.

The display behind you flickers to life, its blue background a portrait to a flashing 3 digits:

THIRD PIECE: 0 – 0 – 6

The creature takes up half the room, suffocating your vision.

Slowly you realize the futility of your efforts.

It encloses you seemingly on all sides, its writhing flesh digging into yours.

You feel its cold embrace as you're engulfed into it.

You can't breathe.

You feel your essence fading away, crushed into the monster's

So many voices fill your head as you join the rest of them.

Bad End
