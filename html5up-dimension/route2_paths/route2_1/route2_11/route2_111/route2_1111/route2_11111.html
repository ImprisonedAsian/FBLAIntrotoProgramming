A Narrow Escape

It pounds on the door, taking its time with each strike.

The calm, terrifying tempo of the clang of metal makes you twitch in fear each impact.

Your hands scramble over the keys, bits of dust scattering as you sift through the glitchy interface.

You click through files and programs, landing on the operations software.

In what seemed like hours, you eventually find the system override. Words appear in big, bold type:

ENTER ACCESS CODE: █ █ █ █

Wracking your brain, you try and find any information from your haunting memories of this place, looking for a 4-digit code.

Where could this have been? On a secret note? A security handbook? One of the thousands of flickering monitors?

Your trembling hands are barely able to hit the keypad as you enter in the code and hold your breath.

For a moment it goes quiet, as if the monster too is reveling in the suspense.

Spectre in the Bright Night

You let out a shivering breath as the hatch clicks open.

Stumbling to the door, you yank it open and throw yourself out into the dark, open world.

Behind you, you hear the thump of heavy metal falling into place.

The echoes of the monster's voice linger in your head.

For now, the threat is contained.

You sit there in the soft grass for what feels like an hour, listening to your ragged breaths.

Weakly, you get to your feet. Looking out at the barren expanse, you know your journey is far from over.

You walk in slow, exhausted steps in a random direction, hoping to find sanctuary in a vehicle or small town.

Your mind swirls with loud, unignorable questions.

The monster has been contained for now, but the relentless hand of time will eventually come to set it free.

Sitting down in a small shelter made from rocks and leaves, you drift in to a restless sleep.

An END.

No One Can Hear You

The screen blares red.

You try again and again, practically clawing at the keypad, screaming at it to set you free.

The monster watches from behind.

Even in its scrambled face, you see its sadistic glee.

You crumple down beside the control terminal. Nothing more can be done.

Looking at it, you smile.

The door bursts open.

Before you could blink, it was already around your neck.

You don't scream. The monster doesn't get that satisfaction.

Liberating pain fills your senses as you crumple down.

BAD END
