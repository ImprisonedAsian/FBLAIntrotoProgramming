Deeper into Darkness

You sprint down the corridor, pushing yourself deeper into the facility. The walls blur past as the flickering emergency lights cast your elongated shadow ahead of you.

Behind you—a screech. The creature is still coming. Faster.

Your legs burn, but you don’t stop. You can’t.

The corridor narrows as you enter a lower sector of the facility. Pipes rattle overhead, steam hissing from ruptured vents. The air is thick, metallic, tinged with something old and rotten.

Then—you see it.

Ahead, the hallway splits into two distinct paths:

To the left, a dark, tight passageway lined with maintenance tunnels. It looks like a maze, but it might be enough to lose the creature.

To the right, a larger corridor leads toward a set of reinforced doors. They’re partially open, the room beyond shrouded in darkness. If you can slip inside, you might find something useful—or trap yourself.

Choice 1: Take the narrow maintenance tunnels – They’re small, but if you navigate them fast enough, you might be able to lose the creature.

Choice 2: Run for the reinforced doors – There could be something inside to help you—or it could be a dead end.
