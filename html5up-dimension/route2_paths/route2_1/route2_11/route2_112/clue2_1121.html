Dead End, New Light

You lunge for the hatch, gripping the rusted edges and pulling yourself through. The metal groans under your weight, and for a terrifying second, it feels like it might collapse beneath you—but it holds.

The moment you’re inside, you slam the hatch shut, twisting the corroded latch into place. A barrier. It won’t hold forever, but it’s something.

You turn, taking in your new surroundings.

It’s a storage chamber, filled with abandoned maintenance equipment, broken monitors, and a single, dimly flickering terminal in the corner.

A soft hum fills the air as you step toward it, dust swirling beneath your footsteps. The screen is shattered, barely functioning, but something glows faintly in the distortion—

"SECOND PIECE: 7 – 2 – 2"

Your breath catches. Another clue.

But then—a sound behind you.

A deep, slow creak.

You whip around, eyes locking onto the hatch. The metal is bulging inward, deep claw marks scraping through the rusted surface.

The creature isn’t stopping.

You spin, searching for another exit—nothing. Just solid walls, thick pipes, and useless debris.

The hatch explodes inward.

A mass of shifting limbs and hollow, gleaming eyes floods into the room. The creature moves unnaturally fast, closing the distance before you can react.

You stumble back, your mind screaming for a way out—but there isn’t one.

The last thing you see is its twisting form engulfing you, its screech drowning out your final breath.
