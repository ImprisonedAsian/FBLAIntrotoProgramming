Fate's laughter

You sprint forward, forcing every ounce of speed into your legs. The hallway stretches endlessly, the dim emergency lights casting erratic, flickering shadows. Behind you, the creature’s claws tear against the metal floor, closing in fast.

Your lungs burn, every step feeling heavier. Then—you see it.

The blast door.

Halfway down the corridor, the reinforced bulkhead looms, a last line of security between you and whatever nightmare is hunting you. It’s still open—but not by much. The emergency system must have partially sealed it during the lockdown. There’s just enough space to squeeze through.

Beyond it, the hallway continues, leading deeper into the facility.

The creature shrieks behind you. It’s faster than you. If you don’t act now—it will catch you.

Choice 1: Slide under the blast door and try to shut it behind you – If you’re fast enough, you might be able to trap it on the other side.

Choice 2: Keep running deeper into the facility – The door might slow it down, but staying ahead is the only thing that matters now.
