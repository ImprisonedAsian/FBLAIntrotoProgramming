You Trusted Echo

You hesitate for only a moment before stepping away from the terminal. You have no idea who wrote the warning—or why—but right now, ECHO is the only one offering a way out.

“I’m trusting you,” you say under your breath.

“Acknowledged,” the AI replies, devoid of emotion.

As you step into the corridor, the door behind you slides shut with a mechanical hiss. The walls are lined with flickering lights, some cracked, some barely holding onto their glow. A faint mist clings to the floor, curling around your ankles as you move forward.

“Head straight and take the second left,” ECHO instructs. “Hurry.”

The urgency in its voice unsettles you. You pick up your pace.

Then, a metallic screech tears through the silence—followed by a deep, guttural growl.

You freeze. The sound came from somewhere ahead. A shadow moves in the dim light, a hunched figure dragging itself along the corridor. You catch a glimpse of twisted limbs, a body that doesn’t move like it should.

“ECHO,” you whisper, barely breathing, “what is that?”

A pause. Then, the AI responds: “Obstacle detected. Adjusting route.”

The thing in the hallway turns its head at the sound of your voice. It hears you.

ECHO's voice is suddenly sharp. "Run."

Choice 1: Sprint to the alternate path ECHO provides and trust it to guide you.

Choice 2: Find a hiding spot and hope the creature doesn’t find you.
