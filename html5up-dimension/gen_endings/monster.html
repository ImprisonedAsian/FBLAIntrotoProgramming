The Final Mistake

Your breath is shallow. Your limbs are frozen. But it doesn’t matter.

The creature has already found you.

A deep, guttural growl rumbles through the air as its twisted form looms over you. Milky-white eyes lock onto yours, unblinking, inhuman. For a fleeting moment, time seems to slow—just long enough for fear to truly set in.

Then, it strikes.

Pain lances through your body as claws tear into flesh. A choked gasp escapes your lips, but it’s already too late. The world tilts, your vision blurring as you are dragged into the darkness.

Somewhere, distant and crackling, you hear ECHO’s voice one last time.

"Failure acknowledged."

Then, everything fades.
