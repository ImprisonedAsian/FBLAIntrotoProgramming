Unleashed

You throw yourself forward, diving beneath the narrowing gap of the blast door just as the creature lunges.

For a moment, you think you’ve escaped.

You slam your palm against the control panel, willing the decayed system to respond. The blast door groans, gears shrieking, before finally slamming shut with a heavy THUD.

Silence.

You stagger back, chest heaving. It’s trapped.

Then—the metal buckles.

Your stomach drops.

A deafening, rage-fueled screech rips through the air as the creature slams against the blast door. The reinforced steel bulges outward, deep claw marks carving through the surface like it’s nothing.

The lockdown system was never meant to hold something like this.

With a final, sickening crunch, the door bursts apart.

You run.

But it’s not chasing you anymore.

Instead, it turns away.

Toward the distant, crumbling exit gates.

The facility’s main security doors—ajar.

Your breath catches in your throat. No.

It shrieks in triumph before launching itself forward, its twisted form moving with horrifying speed.

You sprint after it, but your legs feel like lead. The emergency lights flash red—breach detected—but no alarms will stop it now.

By the time you reach the ruined security gates, it’s already outside.

The air is thick, the sky painted in the dying hues of dusk. The wind howls across the desolate landscape. But the creature doesn’t hesitate.

It disappears into the wilderness.

Your knees buckle. A wave of nausea washes over you as the horrifying truth sinks in.

This facility was never just some abandoned relic. It was a prison.

And you just let the world’s worst nightmare escape.

Somewhere, beyond the hills, past the broken roads and empty towns… people still exist.

They don’t know what’s coming.

But they will.

BAD END
