Darkness Enveloping

Your muscles tense, and in a split second, you make your choice.

You run.

The creature screeches behind you, its claws scraping against metal as it lunges—but you’re already sprinting down the corridor.

"Redirecting. Right turn ahead," ECHO instructs.

You push yourself forward, lungs burning. The floor vibrates beneath you as the thing gives chase, its ragged breath growing louder, closer. You don’t dare look back.

The alternate path appears—a narrow alleyway barely wide enough for you to fit through. You dive inside.

You hear the wretched cries that emanate behind you but keep your eyes forward.

Weaving through the ruinous passage, you find a strong metal door ahead. The monster is gaining on you.

It's dubious you can keep ahead for long. It may be worth it to go through the door and hope for the best.

Choice 1: Open the door and push through to the other side. Hopefully it is strong enough to hold the creature back.

Choice 2: Keep running. You may find something new ahead.
